Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors with System Algorithm!


Objective:


Beat your opponent by choosing the winning move in this classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.


How to Play:


  1. Look for the Rock, Paper, and Scissors buttons on the screen.
  2. Click on the button representing your choice: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  3. The system will randomly choose its move.
  4. The game will determine the winner based on the following rules:
    • Rock beats Scissors
    • Paper beats Rock
    • Scissors beats Paper

Scoring:


  • If you win a round, your score will increase.
  • If the system wins, your opponent's score will increase.
  • In case of a draw, neither score changes.

Keep an Eye on the Scores:


  • Your score is displayed on the left, and your opponent's score is on the right.
  • The game will announce the winner of each round.


Reset the Game:

  • If you want to start over, simply click the "Reset" button. It will reset the scores, and you can begin a new game.

Have Fun:


  • Enjoy the excitement of predicting and outsmarting your opponent in this timeless Rock Paper Scissors game. May the best strategist win!
